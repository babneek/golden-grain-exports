Our Process

Sourcing → Milling → Packaging → Export

Step 01
🌾

Sourcing

Direct procurement from farmer collectives across Karnal, Kurukshetra, Kaithal and Punjab — verified for variety, moisture and purity at the gate.

Step 02
🏭

Milling

Modern stainless-steel mills with rubber-roll shellers, paddy separators, color sorters and length graders. Zero metal contamination.

Step 03
📦

Packaging

Food-grade jute, PP woven & non-woven bags from 5 kg to 50 kg. Custom branding and private-label support.

Step 04
🚢

Export

Containerized shipments via Mundra, Nhava Sheva and Kandla. Documentation, fumigation and inspection handled in-house.
