Karnal — The Rice Bowl of India

Born in the heartland of basmati

Established 25 years ago in the heart of Karnal, Haryana — India's renowned "Rice Bowl" — Gauri Agro has grown from a modest family venture into one of the region's most trusted rice milling enterprises. Founded by visionary entrepreneurs Mr. Gagan Gupta and Mr. Mirdul Gupta, our company represents three decades of agricultural heritage and a quarter-century of milling excellence.

With our state-of-the-art processing facility capable of handling 200 MT daily, we take pride in transforming the finest paddy into premium-quality rice that reaches millions of households worldwide. Our commitment to quality, transparency, and customer satisfaction has made us a preferred partner for importers, wholesalers, retailers, and HORECA customers across global markets.

Modern Technology Meets Traditional Expertise

Advanced Sortex Technology

Our state-of-the-art color sorting machines ensure 100% purity by removing discolored grains, stones, and foreign materials with precision accuracy.

Precision Milling Process

Modern rubber-roll shellers and paddy separators preserve grain integrity while achieving optimal length-to-breadth ratios for premium basmati quality.

Quality Assurance

Multi-stage quality control with length grading, moisture testing, and aroma preservation ensures every batch meets international export standards.

Controlled Aging Process

Our climate-controlled storage facilities allow for proper aging of basmati rice, enhancing aroma and cooking characteristics over 12-24 months.

Processing Capacity

200 MT daily processing capacity at our Village Amritpur Kalan facility with capacity for custom processing, private labeling, and flexible packaging solutions from 1kg to 50kg bags.
