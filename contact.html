FAQ

For International Buyers

What is your minimum order quantity? +

Our standard MOQ is one 20-foot container (approximately 25 MT). We can accommodate smaller LCL shipments for sample trials and new market development.

Which incoterms do you support? +

We work on FOB, CIF, CFR and DAP terms. Our main loading ports are Mundra, Nhava Sheva and Kandla with full container stuffing facilities.

What packaging options are available? +

We offer 1kg to 50kg in PP woven, jute, and non-woven bags. Private labeling and custom branding solutions are available for established buyers.

Do you provide samples before bulk orders? +

Yes, we provide 1-2 kg samples worldwide. All samples are drawn from the same lot that will be shipped for your bulk order.

What certifications do you hold? +

We are FSSAI, APEDA registered with HACCP compliance. Our mill maintains ISO standards for food safety and quality management.

What is the typical lead time? +

10-14 days from order confirmation, including milling, quality testing, fumigation and container stuffing at port.
